What it is

Connection, community, social play, and silly club events.

Event products

Clubs get formats, drills, and invitation prompts, not just a logo.

Score-Help SocialFor players uncomfortable keeping score. Staff leads approachable games and makes score confusion normal.
Baby Block ClassPlayers try to get the ball past the pro from kitchen line to kitchen line while the pro baby blocks and lets out-balls go.
Five-Hole Baby BlockPlayers aim through the five-hole gap and learn that a tiny calm block can beat a big swing.
Dink ForeverPlayers win points by staying soft until someone gets impatient and rolls the dice too soon.
Pro Plays InThe pro joins member games and floats kitchen lobs so players get silly chances to finish easy balls.
Kitchen Sitter LabPlayers practice the ball everyone thinks is automatic, then reset when the sitter still finds a way to get missed.
Middle MysteryTeams earn points for clear calls, coverage, and recovering from "who's got middle?" confusion.
Wiffle Ball Weird WorksPlayers score bonus points for awkward saves, mishits, tweeners, resets, and ugly balls that stay alive.
Invitation RoundAfter the event, players get a simple prompt to invite the right friend back into the next silly format.
