What it is

Connection, community, social play, and silly club events.

Club product

Repeatable let-your-guard-down experiences.

Silly Pickleball gives any club a playbook for lower-level, social-first players: approachable skill classes, pro-led games, welcoming event formats, and simple invitation loops that turn good experiences into word-of-mouth growth.

  • Events and classes designed around silly pickleball moments.
  • Staff scripts and drills clubs can run again and again.
  • An invitation tool that turns happy players into referral sales.
