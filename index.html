Three attempts

Same brand, different center of gravity.

Route 01

Joy-first community brand.

Best for apparel, events, content, and a consumer-facing club movement. The promise is simple: play loose, meet people, come back smiling.

  • Laugh before status shows up.
  • Rotate into new people fast.
  • Make the paddle tap mean something.

Route 02

Programmable club experiences.

Best for NEPC-style events, clinics, social nights, and staff-run rituals. The brand becomes a repeatable format a club can run every week.

  • Silly socials with structured rotation.
  • Beginner-safe games that still feel sharp.
  • Event names people remember.

Route 03

The wedge into the social graph.

Best for the investor story. The silly surface is not the point; it is the mechanism that makes human behavior visible.

  • Who waits, includes, laughs, and invites back.
  • Who makes the room easier to join.
  • Who people want again.
